One Woman's Trash is another Woman's Treasure

A flea market is a type of street market that provides space for vendor to sell previously owned or second hand goods. This type of market is often seasonal. Consistently, there tends to be an emphasis on sustainable consumption whereby items such as used goods, collectibles, antiques and vintage clothing can be purchased.
